About Me

Hello! I am Genniesys Bracia, a passionate web designer dedicated to crafting beautiful, user-friendly, web-based projects. With a keen eye for detail and a love of aesthetics, I major in designing websites that are not only gorgeous to the eye but also give the best user experience. It is through the love of sunsets that I get the urge to transfer the exact beauty, serenity, and warmth evoked by a sunset into what I design.

Just like no two sunsets are alike, so too with each project: I am a never-stop-learning and self-developing individual in the field of web design, I make sure to merge creativity with functionality in every project: the designs should not just catch one's attention but turn out useful for the end-user. I'm a firm believer in design as a powerful tool for conveying ideas or solving problems. I would like to bring even more of my ideas into life by drawing beauty from sunsets to create designs that turn out comfortable and impactful, just like stunning views.
